FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Are the workshops live or recorded?
+
We offer both! Some workshops are live interactive sessions with mentors, while others are pre-recorded and available for self-paced learning. Each workshop listing clearly states its format.
Are the assignments 100% original?
+
Yes, absolutely. Every assignment is written from scratch by our subject matter experts. We run all work through plagiarism checkers before delivery and provide a plagiarism report on request.
Which countries do you serve for assignments?
+
We serve students in 40+ countries including India, UK, USA, Australia, Canada, UAE, Singapore, New Zealand, Malaysia, South Africa, Germany and many more. We work in all time zones.
Will I get a certificate after the workshop?
+
Yes! All TechEra workshops come with a verified certificate of completion issued by TechEra Knowledge and Careers Pvt. Ltd. You can add it to your LinkedIn and resume.
How do I submit my assignment requirements?
+
Simply WhatsApp us or fill the contact form. Share your subject, topic, word count, deadline and any guidelines your university provided. We respond within 30 minutes with a price quote.
Do you offer refunds for workshops?
+
Yes. If you are not satisfied within 24 hours of a live workshop or 7 days of a recorded course, we offer a full refund — no questions asked. Your satisfaction is our priority.
What payment methods do you accept?
+
We accept UPI (PhonePe, GPay, Paytm), Net Banking, Debit/Credit Cards, Razorpay, and international payments via PayPal and Wise for overseas students.
Can I speak to someone before enrolling?
+
Of course! WhatsApp or call us and our counsellors will guide you to the right workshop or service for your goals — completely free. No pressure, just honest advice.
